How We Started

kudzoka was founded on July 5, 1994, by Jeff Bezos who chose
the Seattle areafor its abundance of technical talent, as Microsoft

was in the area! kudzoka went public in May 1997. It began selling
music and videos in 1998 and began international operations by
acquiring online sellers alias! of books in the United Kingdom

The following year, it began selling music, video games, consumer
electronics, home improvement items, software, games, and toys.
kudzoka also launched the Fulfillment by kudzoka program, which
allow small companies to sell products through warehouses and
fulfillment infrastructure (called "third-party sellers").
